About Me

Hello! My name is Vitor, and I'm a freelance front-end web developer. I have been a freelancer for about 2 years, and I build websites for a variety of clients, wether it's for their own brand, business or personal use.

But why do I build websites using code, and not use a website builder you ask? Well, website builders are good for people in a rush. I also like to be efficient and finish a job on time, but I rather continue to develop my own skills as I create amazing websites, than rush a job for quick money!

Why Choose Me?

Responsive Design

Not only will I build you an amazing website, I will make sure it looks perfect on any device! There's no hidding the fact that mobile usage to surf the web has become very popular in recent years, and due to that, any website should look and feel just as good as if you were on a desktop computer.

Reliable and Trustworthy

You might also be thinking, "Why should I choose this random guy to design me a website?", let me give you a few reasons why.

Website builders are quick and cheap, but they're not meant for long-term usage;

Website builders, unlike us developers, have many design and functionality limitations;

You could unnecessarilly spend many, many hours building a website yourself, and regret in the end because it doesn't function like you want it to.
